Why did we pick this topic?

"I've lived all around the world and experienced all levels of hardship and joy. I recognize the enormous privilege I have that largely stem from me winning the lottery of having been born in this country. I feel pain when I see people vilify those in need. I wanted to take a step forward and pull the veil of mystery and fear of "the other." This world needs to be kinder and I feel that this project can do tremendous work to move towards that dream." - Miguel

"I moved to the United States at 15 years old and I got to meet people from different cultures and even though we spoke different languages, we bonded over being “newcomers.” Over the years, I have witnessed how media outlets and political leaders have demonized immigration but my hope is to lend a helping hand in changing public opinion to a more progressive view of the world." - Jessica"

"Growing up as a first generation immigrant, I have witnessed first hand racism, prejudice, and discrimination from people that don’t understand our cultures, people who generalize someone's character based on where they come from and not who they are as a person." -Maggie"
